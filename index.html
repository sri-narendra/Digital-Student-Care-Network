Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UmeedBot

Is UmeedBot a replacement for therapy?

No, UmeedBot is not a replacement for professional therapy. It's designed to provide support, resources, and a listening ear between therapy sessions or for those with mild to moderate mental health concerns. For serious mental health conditions, we always recommend consulting with a licensed mental health professional.

How does UmeedBot protect my privacy?

We take your privacy seriously. All conversations with UmeedBot are encrypted end-to-end, and we never share your personal information with third parties. You can use a pseudonym if you prefer to remain anonymous, and you have full control over your data.

Is UmeedBot free to use?

Yes, UmeedBot offers a free version with access to basic features including chat support, mood tracking, and some guided exercises. We also offer a premium subscription with additional features for those who want more comprehensive support.

Can UmeedBot provide crisis support?

While UmeedBot can provide support during difficult times, it is not a crisis intervention service. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately. UmeedBot can provide you with relevant crisis resources if needed.
