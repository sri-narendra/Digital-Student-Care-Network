About Our Assessment Tools

PHQ-9 (Depression)

A 9-item questionnaire that measures depression severity based on DSM-IV criteria.

GAD-7 (Anxiety)

A 7-item scale that assesses anxiety symptoms and their severity.

GHQ-12 (General Health)

A 12-item screening tool for detecting general psychological distress.

All assessments are confidential and designed to help identify areas where support may be beneficial.

Remember: Your well-being matters. Taking care of yourself improves both personal and professional life.
